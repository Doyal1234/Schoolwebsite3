Garden

Know about Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach, Kolkata: Board & Curriculum, Admission Procedure, Fees & Fee Structure, Contact Details, Facilities and Ranking, Rating and Reviews. Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach Kolkata, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from I to XII. It is an Hindi, English Medium school. The school is located in Garden Reach area of Kolkata, West Bengal. Kendriya Vidyalaya Garden Reach was established in 1988.


Library

It was established in 1672

The library of KV, Lalitpur is growing day by day through acquisition of required books. Currently the library has 1948 books. The library is also equipped with computer system, projector, BCR and bar code printer. For automation the library uses e-Granthalaya software. Library periods are used to bring students to the library for reading and issue/return of the books from circulation counter. The library aims to promote reading habits in students as well as staff of KV, Lalitpur. The library holdings are arranged subject wise for quick retrieval of books. Biographies of several eminent politicians, national heroes, scientists etc are made available to students. Five different Newspapers and twenty one magazines are available in library for staff and students. Book borrow cards are used for issue return to students and register is maintained for issue/return to staff. Facility of internet is also available in the library. A Library Orientation Program is conducted at the beginning of new session for the students of Class Six. Various activity based programs are conducted to create skills among the students.
